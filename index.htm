Study Plan

Time Activity Details
6:00 PM - 6:30 PM Complete Physics Homework Focus solely on homework; set a timer for 30 minutes to avoid distractions.
6:30 PM - 7:15 PM Study Databases Review key concepts and terminology. Use flashcards or notes for quick revision.
7:15 PM - 7:30 PM Break Take a 15-minute break to recharge. Stretch, grab a snack, or drink some water.
7:30 PM - 8:00 PM Study Social Studies Review your notes and key concepts. Identify areas needing more focus for tests or assignments.
8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Meeting Prepare all necessary materials beforehand. Focus on discussion points during the meeting.
9:00 PM - 9:30 PM Research Preparation Research the four concepts needed for your project. Spend about 7-8 minutes on each concept to gather relevant information.
9:30 PM - 10:00 PM Review and Organize Research Notes Organize your findings and make a rough outline for your research project. Highlight key points.
10:00 PM - 10:30 PM Wind Down Prepare for bed, relax, or do a quick review of what you studied today. Consider light reading or mindfulness exercises.
